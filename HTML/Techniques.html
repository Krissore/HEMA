Core Concepts

Kunst des Fechtens is built on principles like timing, distance, and blade control. Below are some of the fundamental techniques taught by historical fencing masters.

Important Techniques

  • Zornhau – The "Wrath Cut", a powerful diagonal cut used both offensively and defensively.
  • Zwerchhau – A horizontal cut used to intercept or dominate an incoming strike.
  • Abnehmen – "Taking away"; a disarming technique after a bind.
  • Winden – "Winding" the blade to gain leverage or change line during a bind.
  • Fühlen – "Feeling" the pressure through blade contact to decide your next move.
  • Durchwechseln – "Changing through", dropping your blade under your opponent's to escape a bind.

Guard Positions (Huten)

  • Vom Tag – "From the Roof", an offensive high guard.
  • Pflug – "Plow", a low guard ready for thrusts.
  • Ochs – "Ox", a high side guard effective for defense and thrusts.
  • Alber – "Fool", a deceptive low guard used to bait attacks.
